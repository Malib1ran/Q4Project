  • María Clara de los Santos y Alba
✧.* Maria Clara, the daughter of Captian Tiago (though it is later stated that her true father is Padre Damaso), is the second main character of Noli Me Tangere.
✧.* She is also the fiancé of Crisostomo Ibarra.
  • Don Santíago de los Santos
╰┈➤Kapitan Tiago is a wealthy Filipino in the village of San Diago, which means the Spaniards and other rich people respect him while those who do not have that kind of money hate him for his pretentiousness.
╰┈➤ He is also introduced as the father of Maria Clara.
  • Dámaso Verdolagas
✟ Padre Damaso is a Fransiscan friar with the personality of an arrogant millionare. He is incredibly physically abusive and corrupt, despite him being a man of God. He is the main antagonist of Noli Me Tangere.
✟ Throughout the story, it is revealed that he sexually assaulted Donya Pia (Maria Clara's mother) and bore the daughter which he calls his goddaughter.
  • Padre Bernardo Salví
♰ Padre Salvi is the new Fransiscan friar of San Diego. Unlike Padre Damaso, though, he abuses through finance, emptying the poor's pockets even more.
♰ He is the one successing after Padre Damaso.
