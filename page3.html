Want to sunbathe next to a beach like the movies? Or perhaps watch how beautiful crops are grown? Well look no further than the Philippines! Consisting of 7,641 islands, this archipelago is famous for its breathtaking beaches and amazing places like Palawan to wind back.

Batad Rice Terraces

Built around 2,000 years ago, these rice terraces have been used as a way to harvest a bountiful amount of rice, a staple in the Philippines and in Asia in general.

Fort Santiago

This bastion is one of the oldest in Manila! It was built by the spaniards due to the fact that they realized that it would be strategic for them to do so.

Basco Lighthouse, Batanes

This lighthouse was built so that it could guide the fishermen at sea. Including this, there are three main lighthouses in Batanes.
